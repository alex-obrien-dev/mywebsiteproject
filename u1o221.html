Trello has met all my needs for project management and gives me a constant visual overview of the projects progress.

For the coding of HTML and CSS I have used Visual Studio Code, its worth noting this software is free and I am aware there are paid alternatives which may have been more time efficient. I found that this program was sufficient, and I have not encountered any issues. The highlight is that its relatively easy to use, even for a beginner coder.

Slack is excellent for communication from team and managers and I have had no problems whatsoever. It enables me to be constantly informed and I can get any queries or questions I have answered with respectable speed.
