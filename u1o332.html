Describe ways to improve productivity and efficiency

There are a multitude of different ways to improve productivity and efficiency. These include but are not limited to, increased training and education on programming and software, investment in tools and software that could save time and decrease the workload. Furthermore, project management can always be improved, most importantly learning from past errors.
